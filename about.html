Cara Curphey.

"I am a people person, and working closely with business stakeholders (whether owners or execs) and watching their businesses grow gives me a great sense of satisfaction. I enjoy giving them the peace of mind to leave the numbers to me so they can concentrate on what they're passionate about."
I am the owner of Albion Consulting. I have over 20 years of accounting experience in both corporate and start-up environments and as a freelancer. ​​Until 2017 I was the Director of Operations at a tech start-up in San Francisco, but when that was acquired I took some time off to think about what came next. I decided that I was happiest when working as a consultant, so decided to go back to my accounting roots and start a consulting practice to help business owners free up valuable time by doing the accounting and back -office heavy lifting.
Originally from Brighton in the UK, I have lived in the US since 2000. My family and I have lived on both the east and west coast, and in the both the south-east and north-west corners of the US. I moved to Mill Valley, CA in 2015 after spending 6 years in Seattle, where I worked as a ​freelance bookkeeper to a diverse portfolio of small business clients. This time around I have found that my start-up experience has led me to have mainly tech based clients: SaaS, AI, wearable tech, or professional service providers to start-ups. I guess it comes with the territory.
